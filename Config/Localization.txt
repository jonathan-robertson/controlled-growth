controlledGrowthTip00,Journal Tip,,,,"7 Days to Die A20 introduced Loot Stage Bonuses based on the Biome a player is in. While this concept of more difficult biomes providing better loot made a lot of sense and was generally welcomed by players, they later found it possible to find end-game gear while exploring the Wasteland as early as level 100. While Wasteland difficulty does scale some, many players feel the Loot Stage Bonus far outpaces the increased risk this biome provides and feel this combo weakens the survival experience.\n\nControlled Growth aims to [ff8000]spread loot across all 300+ levels without removing biome bonuses[-]. [ff007f]You will now be required to venture into higher-difficulty biomes in order to find better gear[-]. [ff8000]The difference in loot quality between biomes grows exponentially[-], [00ff80]rewarding players for venturing out into dangerous territory more and more as they grow - but doing so without breaking the game[-].\n\nIt's recommended to explore the biomes in this order: [5aac5e]Forest[-] -> [ffeba0]Desert[-] -> [dcdcdc]Snow[-] -> [ffab75]Wasteland[-]. At level 300, Tier 3 Gear will drop at high rates in the [ffab75]Wasteland[-], low rates in the [dcdcdc]Snow[-], and rarely in the [ffeba0]Desert[-], even at higher difficulty POIs.\n\nWhile modifiers like the [ff8000]Lucky Looter perk[-], [ff8000]Eye Kandy[-], and [ff8000]Aviator Goggles[-] [00ff80]do still help[-]; their effects have also been reduced."
